## Confirmed EY Products on Guidewire Marketplace

### 1. EY CDA Snowflake Connector for Guidewire
- **Marketplace URL**: https://marketplace.guidewire.com/product/ey-cda-snowflake-connector-for-guidewire/01tPW000007mgSLYAY
- **Also listed as**: EY & Snowflake Insurance Insights Platform for Guidewire
- **Category**: Data / Analytics Integration
- **Description**: Facilitates making the entire Guidewire data set (via Guidewire Cloud Data Access / CDA) available for analysis directly in a Snowflake account. Enables faster deployment of CDA-driven data pipelines, shorter initial load times, and quicker access to change records.
- **Key capabilities**:
  - Full Guidewire dataset exposure to Snowflake
  - Accelerated CDA pipeline deployment
  - Near-real-time change data access for analytics
  - Reduces time-to-analytics for insurers migrating to cloud
